Tourism – Training is being provided to connect with new opportunities for self-reliance and employment.

Women will enable the viewing of Maheshwar through virtual reality.

Dainik Bhaskar Correspondent, Maheshwar

On Tuesday, under the safe tourism site complexes operated for women by the District Administration, MP Tourism Board, and Vasudha Vikas Sansthan, the Virtual Reality (VR) counseling training program was inaugurated at the Tourism Facility Center.

This has been an important initiative in the direction of women's empowerment and employment generation.

Eight women participated in this training. After completion, tourists visiting the Tourism Facility Center and Narmada Ghat will experience virtual tours of the city through VR technology.

Collector Bhavya Mittal recently discussed with Alok Choube, Gender Advisor of MP Tourism Board, about training women and linking them to livelihood opportunities at the Tourist Facility Center.

District Panchayat Technical Assistant Neeraj Amzhare stated that through this initiative, tourists will gain a new digital experience of the city, while local women will receive new opportunities for self-reliance and employment.

Chief Guest Janpad CEO Reena Chauhan said that the efforts being made by MP Tourism Board are commendable.

This will allow tourists to connect deeply with local heritage through modern technology while empowering women to become self-reliant.

During the training program, a presentation of experiences was also conducted. A seven-day training is being conducted by Master Trainers Nitin Soje and Lakhan Balchandani from TellMe Digiinfotech Pvt. Ltd.

Mithun Rawat, Cluster Coordinator of Vasudha Vikas Sansthan, shared information about the progress made under the project.
